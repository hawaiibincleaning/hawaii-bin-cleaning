Questions before launch?

Here are the answers customers usually need before joining the launch list or booking recurring bin cleaning.

What areas will Hawaii Bin Cleaning serve?

We are starting with Oʻahu route planning. The first active service areas will depend on launch-list interest, route density, and scheduling demand.

How does bin cleaning work?

On your scheduled day, you leave empty bins curbside after pickup. The truck-mounted unit lifts the bins, cleans the interior, deodorizes them, and returns them curbside.

Do I need to be home?

No. As long as your bins are empty, accessible, and curbside, service can usually be completed without you being home.

Which bins can be cleaned?

Trash, recycling, and green waste bins can be included. Customers can choose the bin mix that fits their household once service plans are live.

Will pricing be available right away?

Pricing is currently hidden while launch testing is underway. Join the launch list to receive founder updates and early service details.

Can this connect to Jobber or Jobatory?

Yes. The website includes booking and customer portal placeholders. Replace the links in assets/js/site-config.js once the customer platform is selected.

What if my bin is not emptied yet?

Like trash collection, the service depends on accessible empty bins. If a bin is full or blocked, it may need to be skipped or rescheduled depending on the route policy.

Do you offer pressure washing?

Pressure washing add-ons can be offered for trash pad areas, sidewalk sections, driveway areas, and other small concrete surfaces based on availability and quote details.
