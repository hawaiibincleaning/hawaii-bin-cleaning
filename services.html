Our process

Professional system. Cleaner curbside experience.

The service is centered around a dedicated truck unit with dual lifters, branded for visibility and built to make bin cleaning efficient.

  • Bins are serviced curbside after trash collection.
  • Hydraulic lifters invert bins for interior cleaning.
  • The cleaning workflow is designed to reduce driveway mess.
  • Bins are deodorized before being returned.
