Hawaii Bin Cleaning respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we may collect, how we use it, and how customers can contact us about privacy questions.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact information such as name, email, phone number, and service address.
  • Service details such as number of bins, service preferences, notes, route information, and customer messages.
  • Payment and billing information handled by third-party payment or scheduling platforms.
  • Website data such as browser type, device information, pages visited, and analytics data if analytics are enabled.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to respond to requests, schedule service, manage customer accounts, process payments, send service updates, improve the website, and communicate about launch availability, offers, and route openings.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use platforms such as Jobber, Jobatory, Urable, Formspree, Google Forms, email marketing tools, analytics tools, and payment processors. Those services process information according to their own privacy policies.

4. Cookies and Analytics

This site may use cookies or analytics tools to understand website traffic and improve the experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with vendors and service providers who help operate the website, booking system, payment processing, customer communication, or service delivery.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect customer information, but no website or internet transmission is completely secure.

7. Your Choices

You may request updates to your information, opt out of marketing communications, or ask privacy-related questions by contacting us.

8. Children

Our website and services are intended for adults and are not directed to children under 13.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@hawaiibincleaning.com.
